Goals for this site

I want this site to be around for a while, to showcase my stuff and remind me of what I loved when I made it. To accomplish this I want to:
  • Maintain the domain for the remainder of my life, and maintain hosting on the site.
  • Keep an active git history for every major subdomain (excluding cdn/library hosting/linking or other non-content pages)
  • Reduce dependencies on other sites and host libdata on site whenever posable
I want to make this site friendly and I want it to look cool to me. I don't and won't care if my site is widely considered pretty, and I will not support mobile devices.

Goals for myself

In addition to my attempt to create a record of my life, and a place to express myself I also want a place to learn about web development and relationships and fandom, to do that specifically I want to:
  • Work on other sites (like https://squll.net) that have other goals, technical requirements, and styles
  • Join or create a webring
  • Collaberate with my in-person and online friends.
I hope to add to this page over time, because this project is ever shifting. See ya in the end times, and DFTBA
