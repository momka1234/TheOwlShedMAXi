What is a broken me?

This is an amorphus and hard to define thing; in short, sometimes in stressful environments (like schools, friends bedrooms, and malls) I will "shut-down" and stop talking. Sometimes I will also:

  • Curl into a ball.
  • Have muscle spasms.
  • Cry (quietly or loudly, the choice is mine!)

What Should You NOT Do?

Broadly, TOUCH ME. While cuddles are really, really, really nice most of the time I find them really stressful when I'm "broken". Additionally asking me to do things, or to leave a place will scare me more. Please don't shout either; I can control it about as much as you can so expecting me to not have it happen is pretty dumb.

What Should you do?

Talk calmly and gently. Ask if I want to go somewhere quiter and if I do lead me there, don't be forceful though. You could also do something on your own; like watch tictok read a book or just have a fun time.

I'm Unbroken, Should You do Anything Special
